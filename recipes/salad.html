Salad

Recipe Description:

Pasta salad is a refreshing dish that combines cooked pasta with a variety of fresh vegetables, herbs, and often proteins like meats or cheeses.
It's typically dressed with a vinaigrette or creamy dressing, offering a colorful and flavorful medley of textures and tastes. Perfect for picnics,
potlucks, or as a side dish, pasta salad is versatile and can be customized to suit different preferences and occasions.

ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain the pasta
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
