Lasagna

Recipe Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich, savory fillings.
Typically, these layers include a robust meat or vegetable-based ragu, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese,
and a generous amount of grated cheese such as mozzarella and Parmesan. The assembled dish is baked until
bubbling and golden brown, resulting in a hearty, comforting meal with a delightful combination of textures
and flavors. Lasagna is a versatile dish, often adapted with various ingredients to suit different tastes
and dietary preferences.

ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
